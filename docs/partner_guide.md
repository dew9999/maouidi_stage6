# Maouidi Partner User Guide

## Becoming a Partner

1. Go to Settings > "Become a Partner".
2. Fill in your professional details (Specialty, License Number).
3. Submit for verification. Once approved, you can access the Partner Dashboard.

## Partner Dashboard

### Managing Schedule

1. **Working Hours**: Set your start and end times for each day in Settings.
2. **Closed Days**: Mark specific dates as closed (e.g., holidays) to prevent
   bookings.
3. **Booking System**: Choose between "Time Slots" (specific times) or "Queue
   Based" (daily limit).

### Handling Appointments

- **New Requests**: View incoming booking requests.
- **Check-in**: Mark patients as "Checked In" when they arrive.
- **Complete**: Mark appointments as "Completed" after the visit. This allows
  patients to leave reviews.

### Homecare Specifics

- **Price Negotiation**: For homecare requests, you can propose a custom price
  based on the case description.
- **Payment**: Earnings are tracked in the "Earnings & Payouts" dashboard.

## Earnings & Payouts

- Access the **Payouts Dashboard** from Settings > Professional > Earnings &
  Payouts.
- View your total revenue, platform fees, and net income.
- Payouts are processed weekly to your registered bank account (CCP/BaridiMob).

## Profile & Reputation

- Keep your profile updated with a clear photo and bio.
- Encourage happy patients to leave 5-star reviews to boost your visibility.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I change my homecare price?** A: Go to Settings > Booking
Configuration > Homecare Service Price.

**Q: Can I pause bookings temporarily?** A: Yes, toggle "Accepting Appointments"
to OFF in Settings.
