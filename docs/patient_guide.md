# Maouidi Patient User Guide

## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Open the app and tap "Create Account".
2. Enter your email and password.
3. Verify your email via the link sent to your inbox.
4. Complete your profile (Name, DOB, Gender, Wilaya).

### Logging In

- Use your email and password to log in.
- If you forget your password, tap "Forgot Password?" to reset it.

## Booking Appointments

### Clinic Appointments

1. Navigate to the Home tab.
2. Search for a doctor or clinic by specialty or name.
3. Use filters to refine by location or rating.
4. Select a provider and tap "Book Appointment".
5. Choose a date and time slot.
6. Confirm your booking.

### Homecare Services

1. Search for "Homecare" providers or select the category.
2. Request a service by selecting a date.
3. **Negotiation**: The partner may propose a price. You will receive a
   notification.
   - **Accept**: Moves the booking to payment/confirmation.
   - **Reject**: Cancels the request.
4. **Payment**: For homecare, you may need to pay an upfront fee securely via
   the app (Chargily).

## Managing Appointments

- View your upcoming and past appointments in the **Dashboard** tab.
- **Cancel**: You can cancel pending appointments. Refunds usually process
  within 7 days.
- **Reviews**: After an appointment is completed, tap "Write Review" on the
  appointment card to rate your experience.

## Profile & Settings

- Go to the **Settings** tab to:
  - Update your profile photo and details.
  - Change language (English, Arabic, French).
  - Toggle Dark Mode.
  - Contact Support.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I book for someone else?** A: Yes, during booking, check "Book for
someone else" and enter their details.

**Q: Is my data secure?** A: Yes, Maouidi uses industry-standard encryption and
secure database storage.
